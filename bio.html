A brief biosketch

Kshitij was born in a small village in the Giridih district of Jharkhand, which is known for its abundance of natural resources. Following his primary, secondary, and higher secondary education in his hometown, Kshitij earned his B.Sc. from St. Xavier's College Ranchi, Jharkhand. He then went to the Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur to pursue M.Sc. in Mathematics. From July 2018 to July 2023, Kshitij spent his time at the Indian Institute of Technology Delhi in pursuit of Ph.D. in Mathematics.
